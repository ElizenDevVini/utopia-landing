Own land in a city that pays you.

utopia is a digital city, sold block by block. Every block you own earns yield backed by tokenized stocks. Built for Robinhood Chain.

how it works

How it works

  1. 1

    Buy a block

    Every plot in utopia is a deed. One block, one owner, recorded on chain.

  2. 2

    The land works

    Each plot is backed by a basket of tokenized stocks. When the basket earns, the land earns.

  3. 3

    Yield stays with the deed

    Hold the block and collect the APY. Sell it, and the yield goes with it.

Built for Robinhood Chain

utopia is built for Robinhood Chain, Robinhood's Arbitrum-based network for tokenized stocks. The rails that put equities on chain are the rails that back the land. utopia deploys when the chain opens to builders.

utopia is a concept. Nothing here is live, for sale, or earning anything yet. No wallet, no token, no waitlist. An idea, drawn in blocks.
