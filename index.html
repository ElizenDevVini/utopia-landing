How it works

  1. 1

    Buy a block

    Every plot in utopia is a deed. One block, one owner, recorded on chain.

  2. 2

    The treasury streams rewards

    Each plot accrues one Stock Token at a disclosed base rate. Payouts depend on the visible reward reserves.

  3. 3

    Rewards stay with the deed

    Unclaimed rewards follow the plot when the deed transfers. The displayed rate is a reference streaming rate, not investment APY.

Every block bought is a block built. Deeds mint as blocks. The skyline is the ledger of who owns what.

Built on Robinhood Chain

Robinhood Chain is Robinhood's network for tokenized assets. Utopia's current prototype reads real contract state from its testnet.

Questions

Is any of this live?
On testnet, yes. The grid above is real contract state. Test assets cost nothing and have no value; mainnet is not deployed.
How do I try it?
Get test ETH for gas, open the dashboard, and select an empty plot. The dashboard shows the configured payment asset and live reward-reserve status.
What backs the rewards?
Only Stock Tokens already held by the land contract can fund payouts. The dashboard exposes those finite reserves and reports any unpaid debt after a claim.
Why Robinhood Chain?
Because that is where the tokenized stocks live. Building the land on the rails that hold the equities keeps the whole loop on one chain.

utopia runs on Robinhood Chain's testnet. Test ETH and testnet Stock Tokens have no value. Mainnet is not deployed. Not an offer of anything.
